Introduction.

Inspired by early nineteenth-century woodblock printing techniques, Brian Bonislawsky, founder of Astigmatic One Eye Typographic Institute, created Ultra serif. This blocky and bold typeface emits a serious, yet friendly personality.

Ultra is an attention-grabbing serif font that stands out from the crowd. Its versatile design makes it suitable for a wide range of applications, from corporate branding and magazine titles to anywhere one wants to make a lasting impression.
